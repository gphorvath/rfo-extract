41.702 Formats for annual utility service review.

(a) Formats for use in conducting annual reviews of the following utility services are available from GSA at energy@gsa.gov and may be used at the agency's discretion:

(1) Electric service.

(2) Gas service.

(3) Water and sewage service.

(b) Contracting officers may modify the annual utility service review format as necessary to fully cover the service used.
