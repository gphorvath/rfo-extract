14.304-2 Apparent clerical mistakes.

(a) The contracting officer may correct any clerical mistake, apparent on its face in the bid, before award. The contracting officer first must obtain from the bidder a verification of the bid intended. Examples of apparent mistakes are obviousâ€”

(1) Misplacement of a decimal point;

(2) Incorrect discounts (for example, 1 percent 10 days, 2 percent 20 days, 5 percent 30 days);

(3) Reversal of the price f.o.b. destination and price f.o.b. origin; and

(4) Mistake in designation of unit.
